Overnight Oats

Description

This overnight oats recipe is a sweet source of high protein and carbs that can be prepared in advance and stored for 4 days refridgerater.

Ingredients

  • Pea Protein Powder
  • Oat Milk
  • Creatine Monohydrate
  • Blueberries
  • Sugar-free Syrup
  • Chia Seeds
  • Rolled Oats
  • peanutbutter powder

Steps

  1. Add 3/4 cups of rolled oats to a mason jar
  2. Combine 1 cup of oat milk, 5 grams of creatine monohydrate, 4 tablespoons of peanutbutter powder, and 4 tablespoons of sugar-free syrup to a blender and blend until a smooth consistency is achieved.
  3. Add the blended items to the jar and stir in the oats to mix evenly.
  4. Top with blueberries and chia seeds
  5. Store refridgerated for no more than 4 days.
