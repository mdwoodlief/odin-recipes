Chocolate Chip Pancakes

Description

This pancake recipe makes dairy-free, high protein pancakes with melted chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • Pea Protein Powder
  • Gluten-free pancake mix
  • Dairy-free chocolate chips
  • Oat Milk
  • Sugar-free syrup
  • Strawberry jam
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Combine 10 scoops of protein powder and 4 cups of gluten-free pancake mix in a mixer and blend to homogeneity
  2. Add 4 cups of oat milk, 1/2 cup o sugar-free syrup, 1/4 cup of strawberry jam, and 4 eggs to the mixer and blend well.
  3. Heat on a skillet over medium heat, flipping as necessary.
  4. Top with chocolate chips
