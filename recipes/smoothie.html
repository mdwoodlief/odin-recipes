High Protein Smoothie

Description

This smoothie contains more than 50 grams of protein per serving and is a dairy-free and gluten-free friendly dish. It contains strawberries and sugar-free syrup for added sweetness.

Ingredients

  • Pea Protein Powder
  • Oat Milk
  • Creatine Monohydrate
  • Frozen Strawberries
  • Sugar-free Syrup

Steps

  1. Combine 1 cup of oat milk and 2 scoops of pea protein powder
  2. Add 1/2 cup of frozen strawberries
  3. Add 1/4 cup of sugar-free syrup
  4. Add 5 grams of creatine monohydrate
  5. Blend to a smooth consistency is achieved
