Deviled Eggs

Description

These popular little finger foods are just so easy to love. And, lucky us, they're almost as easy to make as they are to gobble up. Here's how to make the best deviled eggs, from boiling or hard-cooking the eggs to stuffing them with creative fillings.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • White Vinegar

Steps

  1. Hard boild the eggs.
  2. Peel the eggs under cold, running water. Slice the eggs lengthwise into mirror-image halves. Pop out the yolks with your fingers or spoon them out and into a bowl. Reserve the whites (cups-up) on a plate, covered with a damp paper towel.
  3. Give the yolks a quick preliminary mashing with a fork. For basic deviled eggs, add mayonnaise and mustard, salt and pepper. Optionally, add white vinegar
  4. Scoop the filling into a piping bag, and then pipe the filling back into the empty wells of the egg whites.
  5. Chill the deviled eggs in the fridge for at least 20 minutes and then serve.
